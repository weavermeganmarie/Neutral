Neutral Mobile App Case Study

The Problem:
Individuals often neglect to account for the environmental impacts of their travel choices, despite moral desires.

The Solution:
Neutral is an app that enables individuals who wish to reduce their carbon footprint while traveling, compare options while creating their travel itineraries and offset inevitable emissions.

My Role:

02. Definition & Synthesis

Problem Statement

Neutral App was designed to achieve an easy way to plan a carbon neutral trip.
We have observed that existing products or services aren’t providing a way to calculate and compare the carbon emissions of different methods of transportation and a way to offset those emissions by supporting local carbon negative initiatives without consulting multiple resources, which is causing individuals to travel less sustainably despite their moral desires.
How might we improve Neutral App so that our customers are successful based on their ability to travel carbon neutral without paying significantly more and without significant deviation from their typical vacations/trips.

03. Ideation

In a group, a diversity of “I like”, “I wish”, and “what if” statements were used to brainstorm features. These ideas were then organized by impact and effort. Low effort, high impact ideas were prioritized.

05. User Testing & Outcomes

Iterations based on User Tests

User Tests were conducted on the clickable prototype and iterations were made based on success and feedback.

Pain Point

The onboarding process was a bit slower than desired if you just want to play around with trip ideas.

Solution

Login through Google, Facebook, or Apple. Login as guest.

Pain Point

Users had difficulty distinguishing what the different values and Icons represented in the Trip Planning feature.

Solution

Made some introductory slides that will appear the first time a user plans a trip.

Incorporating UI Elements

A simple Style Guide was developed to begin incorporating more visually pleasing elements.

06. Conclusions & Future Opportunities

Conclusions

Users are willing to travel carbon neutral if there isn’t a significant cost increase. Existing apps do not currently meet users needs. Carbon offsetting is a relatively inexpensive way to reduce carbon footprint. Neutral App provides a simple and efficient way to plan carbon neutral trips, and a network of like minded individuals to give support and advice.

Future Opportunities

Partner with nonprofit carbon negative intiatives.
Incorporate map feature where users can see their route.
More sharing capabilities so users feel recognized for thier efforts.
